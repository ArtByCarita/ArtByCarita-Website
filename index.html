✦ About Me ✦

Behind ArtByCarita

📁 about_carita.txt

ArtByCarita is a UK-based artist brand created by illustrator and designer Carita Whyte. My work is inspired by anime, gaming culture, Y2K aesthetics, cloudcore visuals, retro technology, and original storytelling.

Through ArtByCarita, I create digital illustrations, character art, fanart, merchandise, branding, event graphics, commissions, and original projects. My goal is to build a creative brand that feels personal, imaginative, and memorable.
